Divorced and Happy

Divorce doesn't have to leave you, emotionally crippled, mentally imbalanced, and financially stuck in a pit of despair.

In this book Kathlyn shares a step-by-step approach to overcoming post-divorce obstacles, becoming incredibly happy, and finding fulfillment as you journey towards that destination of self-actualization.

The first place to begin she says "is in your mind. "Change your lens and adjust your focus to adopt the lifestyle of your dreams".

About The Author

Kathlyn Eyitemi

Kathlyn is a trained social worker whose work with GBV survivors has spanned over ten years.

Aside from being a DV survivor herself, her motivation to write this book stems from all the experiences she garnered over the years coaching women who have survived different forms of abuse to regain their footing and take back control of their lives.
